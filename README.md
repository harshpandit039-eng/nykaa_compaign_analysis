#Nkyaa_Campaign_analysis (Dashboard)
Excited to share my latest Data Analytics project!
Project: Nykaa Campaign Sales Analysis Dashboard
I built an interactive Power BI dashboard to analyze marketing campaign performance and uncover actionable business insights.
Key Metrics:
Average ROI: 2.68
Total Clicks: 22.53K
Total Conversions: 22.53K
Total Revenue: 46249M
Key Insights:
Impressions are very high compared to conversions, indicating scope for improving conversion strategies.
YouTube & WhatsApp channels drive the highest conversions, making them the most effective platforms.
Influencer and Email campaigns generate the highest revenue among all campaign types.
Engagement rate remains fairly consistent across campaigns.
Monthly Revenue Analysis:
Revenue started strong in January (~4019M) and showed a gradual decline till April (~3881M).
A spike is observed in May (~3967M), followed by a sharp drop reaching the lowest point in July (~3640M).
From August onwards, revenue steadily recovered, peaking again in November (~3913M).
Slight decline observed in December (~3803M).
Overall Trend:
The data shows a mid-year dip and strong recovery in the later months, indicating possible seasonality or campaign effectiveness in Q4.
What I Learned:
End-to-end marketing funnel analysis (Impressions → Conversions)
Time-series analysis to identify trends and seasonality
Identifying high-performing channels and campaigns
Creating interactive dashboards with Power BI
Tools Used:
Power BI | Data Cleaning | Data Modeling | DAX | Data Visualization
This project enhanced my ability to convert raw data into actionable insights and business decisions.
